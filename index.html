About me

My name is Joonas, I'm 20 years old and taking an education as a graphic designer. I'm a young fella, with a passion for graphic design. Drawing has always been an interest of mine, I first started using Photoshop in 2014; when i was in 8th grade and 14 years old.

I didn't really know what a graphic designer was, until a friend of my mom's recommended it, because he knew about my interests in Photoshop and drawing. So I went directly from elementary school to introductory course 1 at NEXT, which I'm very happy about. The part with working with graphics of introductory course 1, was very easy for me, because I'd already learnt all the basics.

Yeetus that Feetus

I applied to introductory course graphic designer, but I wasn't accepted; then I applied to sign painter instead of being devastated. Introductory course 2 sign painter gave me more of an interest, because the challenges went from Photoshop to Illustrator. I opened my arms wide open for Illustrator, and got the basics a little fast, because I already knew them in Photoshop.

Introductory course 2 sign painter gave me knowledge of typography and creating signs, and a decent expertise in Illustrator. Because of that, I'm actually happy that I didn't get in in the first place.

How to have sex

Introductory course 2 graphic designer has gotten me a bit further, because then I started learning about InDesign, HTML/CSS and a little Animate. At this day I'm standing even stronger, as I've learned how to use Blender and After Effects. But despite my confidence I know there's still lots to learn, and I'm looking forward to my next challenge.

I've gotten the experience at main course 1, to create something for a firm that isn't just fictive. With creating something for a real firm, comes responsibility that can give the jobs a little more excitement. You have to consider what the firm wants, what others see and what it tells people.

There's a lot more to graphic design than just lines and colors, like creating something, sending a message or giving a feeling; and that's what I like about it. The other side of me does a lot. I'm a former gymnast, parkour practitioner, and cheerleader; and once in a while I still like to jump around and do flips. Besides being an acrobat I also like to play video games with my friends.
